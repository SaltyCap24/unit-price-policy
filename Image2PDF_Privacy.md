# Privacy Policy for Image to PDF Converter

Last updated: April 11, 2026

SNODevelopment58 ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Image to PDF Converter (package ID: com.snodevelopment58.image2pdfezconvert) and related services.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to the Image to PDF Converter Android app published by SNODevelopment58.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
- Images/files you select in the app to create PDF documents
- In-app actions such as creating, compressing, and sharing PDFs
- Messages you send when contacting support

### B. Automatically Collected Information

- App diagnostics and error data generated by the Android platform or integrated libraries
- Network-related technical information needed for ad delivery and billing verification

### C. Purchases and Store Data
- If you buy "Remove Ads" or other in-app products, transaction handling is performed by Google Play Billing.
- We do not receive your full payment card details.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide core app features (image-to-PDF conversion, compression, sharing)
- Maintain app stability, security, and performance
- Process and verify in-app purchases
- Show ads in the free experience (where applicable)

- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Local Processing and File Handling

Image to PDF Converter is designed to process your selected files on your device. Your selected images and generated PDFs are handled locally unless you explicitly share/export them using your device's sharing options.

## 5. Permissions Used

Depending on Android version and app features, the app may request:
- READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: to read images you select
- WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (legacy Android versions): to save exported files
- INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: for ads, billing, and related network operations
- AD_ID permission: used by Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising functions

You can manage permissions in your device settings.
